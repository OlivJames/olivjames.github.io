Home of the EXPO mech series.

Action Potential Expression (APEX) is the galaxy's first company focused on the creation of noncombatant mechs.

The EXPO line is designed to enable creativity, discovery, and peaceful progress.

Our mechs are explorers, scientists, and artists - because you are too. Discover an EXPO today with a virtual tour.

Features

  • Unique control system featuring integrated haptic and neural user input
  • Chassis is entirely airtight, enabling use in anaerobic environments from deep ocean to airless exoplanets.
  • Quasi-artificial intelligence allows your EXPO to communicate and exercise limited autonomy while in action.
  • Interchangeable skins for the external panels facilitate camouflage wherever you are. (Sold Seperately)
  • EXPOs can and will sneeze. It's adorable.
  • Secondary arms are made of flexible steel filament, enabling 360-degree reach of up to 15 metres.
  • Shoulder mounted high-frequency photon cannons function as nonlethal stun weapons or flashlights, depending on the situation.
