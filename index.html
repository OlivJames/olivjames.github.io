Founded by three friends and two cousins, APEX believes mechs have immense potential outside of their usual military applications.

Our team is passionate about robotics, electrical engineering, and biomechatronics. What started as a final project in an intro

robotics course became a lifelong journey of experimentation and passion. Above all, we love our mechs, and hope you will too.
